GKSK FOUNDATION




GURU KRUPA SHIKSHANA KENDRAM which is popularly known as GKSK FOUNDATION is a non governamental organization based in hyderabad.

Since 2014 the organization is working with the aim " TO MAKE EDUCATION AND ARTS AFFORDABLE TO EVERYONE."


SHIKSHA KENDRA

The SHIKSHA KENDRA is one of the major projects lead by the GKSK FOUNDATION. Under the shelter of this project the GKSK FOUNDATION is starting schools for the underprileged children.

As a part of this project we have started our first school in the academic year 2022-2023.
The speciality of our schools is that we only charge RS. 5000/- for the whole year as annual fees for any class from NURSERY TO XTH .



The schools under SHIKSHA KENDRA are managed by the team of well qualified dignitories and the staff in our schools is also well qualified and they have years of experience under their hats.

Instead of focusing on only academics we aim to develop the students in all the activites and our curriculam consists of physical activities like TAEKWONDO, YOGA, KARRASAMU AND DANCE along with this for mental sharpness we organize chess , meditation classes and orientation classes by well known personalities from different industries.

DISHA CENTRE


The DISHA CENTRE was started by the GKSK FOUNDATION AS A PART OF SLUM DEVELOPMENT.

In this project The GKSK FOUNDATION conducts free evening tution centres in slum areas of Hyderabad.

In the tution centre we also organizes different activities along with academics.
CLICK HERE TO VIEW DISHA CENTRE PHOTOS


The GKSK FOUNDATION also distributes all the necessary items to the children which will help them in their education such as bags, books, school uniform and stationary items.

Here we organizes different competetions and the winners are supported in terms of finance for their higher education.

Annually more than 1000 students are being sheltered under the DISHA CENTRE project in and around 20 slums of hyderabad.

KALA CENTRE


The KALA CENTRE projects was started to bring the glory to the ancient indian arts back through increasing the awareness in todays generation.

In KALA CENTRE we organizes our martial arts academies at different centres.

Here KALA means ART and CENTRE means HUB and our KALA CENTRE is a place where we teach different arts to the children including TAEKWONDO, YOGA, KARRASAMU, PAINTING, CLAY ARTS AND MANY MORE.

Watch our kala centre video.



Our students shown their talents at different stages and won several medals including gold in national tournaments of TAEKWONDO and YOGA.

The KALA CENTRE projects comprises of 5 hubs in hyderabad which have more than 600 students taking the classes.
WOMEN EMPOWERMENT


Despite watching the situation of women in present society. We wanted to started something that ensures their safety.

Keeping this in mind we have started self defence camps in women colleges.

We provide certified self defence course to the students of BEGUMPET WOMEN COLLEGE AND ST. ANNS WOMEN COLLEGE MEHDIPATNAM.

Under this project we have trained more than 5000 women and provided them with the merit certificates.

HEALTH AND HYGIENE


This project was taken up to improve the health of the people living in slums.

In this project we started providing sanitary pads to the women of the slum areas.

For the children and senior citizen we organizes health camps and provide free medication.

Every month more than 500 women are provided with the sanitary pads under this project.

ABOUT US
